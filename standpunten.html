Points of view

Background check for immigration

A check for every form of immigration whether it is necessary for the client to come to our country. Is there a chance of success in our society and society for the immigrant. We also want to establish a stronger immigration system in the Netherlands, as can already be seen in Australia with the strict visa policy. The Netherlands itself decides who lets it in without the influence of other European countries and powers.

Nexit, the Netherlands to leave the EU

We stand for sovereignty for the Dutch state, that is why we want to leave the EU, it brings us too many financial obligations in connection with with other countries that do not have it in order themselves. We also want the guilder to be reintroduced, one of the most powerful and beautifully valuable currency ever. Back to the strong Netherlands that it once was!

Binding referedum

Democracy in our country has weakened enormously in recent decades. For that reason, we need to empower citizens and not political bodies. This through direct democracy and binding referendums. For people who do not have the option to physically go to the polls, there should be the option to vote online through E-democracy. However, this requires enough privacy on the internet (point 6.).

Integration

Upon admission to Dutch as an immigrant (point 1.), a Dutch course is compulsory for adults. Children and adolescents will learn Dutch in education and, where necessary, receive extra help to master the language. Dutch is the language of communication in every public body and space, with the exception of places of worship. Here may be prayed in the language of faith and this will be respected.

Reintroduction basic study grant

The reintroduction of the basic grant in our country would give much less stress to students at a young age because they are in debt. Thanks to this start-up capital, more young people will do further education. In contrast to the promised 10,000 from Mr. Klaver, this money will provide for certainty and thus a higher educated Netherlands in the future.

Better protection for private data

The private data of the residents of our country should be more protected. We must defend ourselves better against data breaches and actively combat them. The government should have less access to data and only use it for matters of the police or the Dutch secret service. Also, the exchange of private data with other countries must be stopped.

More money to defense

Pay more attention and more money to the defence in the Netherlands. This will lead to an abundance of new forces in the defense of our country, both technically and militarily. As the Netherlands, we must build a strong enough army and navy to defend ourselves against the influence and oppression of the European Union.

the rent must be drastically reduced

The rent of houses and buildings in our country has increased considerably in recent years. This gives starters and new families fewer options to find a house. Also, there are too few homes available on the market. This problem of housing shortage must also be solved.

Environmental measures

We want to get rid of all the useless and superfluous climate measures and agreements that have been made by the EU in recent years. There are much better alternatives such as preserving nature in our country. This allows us to stop unnecessary things like climate zones and operation "van het gas af".

Protection national SME (MKB)

Protecting national companies, supporting them and giving them priority over multinationals and other large foreign companies. The rules for SMEs in our country must become more flexible for dismissal and benefits.
